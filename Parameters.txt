This document explains the parameters used for screen time fatigue detection.

## Parameters
- Blink Rate
- Eye Closure Duration
- Head Posture

#Blink Rate Thresholds for Eye Fatigue Detection

## Normal Blink Rate: 15–20 blinks per minute

## Eye Strain Threshold: Greater than 30 blinks per minute

## System Action: Automatically reduce screen brightness to minimize eye strain.

- Normal Blink Rate: 15–20 blinks/min
- Eye Strain Blink Rate: >30 blinks/min
- Action: Reduce screen brightness
